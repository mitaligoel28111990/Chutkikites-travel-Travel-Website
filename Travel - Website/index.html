Chutki KITES is a transformative initiative designed for girls under 18, in partnership with Katha. We provide comprehensive digital training that equips young girls with essential skills, including document management, graphic design using Canva, spreadsheet creation with MS Excel, and crafting impactful presentations with MS PowerPoint. Beyond these foundational tools, we also focus on boosting their self-confidence by introducing them to innovative platforms like Scratch and ChatGPT, enabling them to explore the latest technologies and unlock their creative potential.
